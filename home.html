I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Astronomy, Astrophysics and Space Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Indore.

My research interests include circumgalactic medium in massive galaxies and its role in galaxy evolution, cluster cosmology/astrophysics with hydro-dynamical simulations and galaxy evolution in dense cluster environments using multi-wavelength data-sets.
